About Us

Our customer’s health is of the utmost importance to us which is why we have taken measures for your safety including multiple hand sanitizer stations, plexi-glass at the manicure station, extra cleaning and sanitization of beds/rooms and high touch surfaces.

Everyone in our salon must be masked at all times and we ask that you bring a mask with you to wear throughout the entire time in the salon. If you forget to bring our own mask, we have masks available for purchase of $1.00. We also ask that you fill out health declaration upon entering the premises.

For the time being we will be shortening our operating hours to the following:
Tuesday 9:30 am to 6:00 pm | Saturday 9:30 am to 4:00 pm

Please remember that services are by appointment only and can be booked though email at info@elsasesthetics.com or by calling (905) 631-6605

We hope to see you soon, Staff of Elsa’s Esthetics
